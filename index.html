About Me

A journey through technology and cultural exchange

Background

My journey in technology began during my early years at AISB in Budapest, where I developed a strong foundation in international education and computer science. After seven enriching years in Hungary, I spent a year in Germany furthering my technical skills before returning to Budapest to pursue my BSc in Computer Science at ELTE University.

I specialize in full-stack development and artificial intelligence, with a particular interest in creating innovative solutions that bridge cultural gaps through technology.

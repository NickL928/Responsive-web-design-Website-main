My Story

As a Chinese student pursuing Computer Science at ELTE University in Budapest, I bring a unique perspective to software development. My journey in programming began in high school, where I discovered my passion for creating digital solutions that make a difference.

What I Do

I specialize in full-stack development, with a focus on creating intuitive and efficient web applications. My experience spans from building educational platforms to developing algorithm visualization tools. I'm particularly interested in educational technology and ways to make learning more accessible and engaging.

Beyond Coding

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring Budapest's historic streets, participating in hackathons, or mentoring junior programmers. I believe in the power of technology to bridge cultural gaps and create meaningful connections across borders.
